INTRODUCTION

As a member of this world, we should devote our efforts to help others in need.

“The more you give, the greater you receive”, just like the meaning of this saying. We don’t have to do anything which exceeds our ability, but we can do our part by donating things which still work but useless for ourselves.

Therefore, let’s take action now, and also be comfortable to look for what you need. Used to be junior/senior high school students, so we highly understand the dilemma of students. Not to mention those in poor family, so we come up with this idea to set up a website for them. Through this website, they can know the immediate information of free resourse of books, food, and diverse events.

Gathering

We hope that our program not only collect support goods, books, or food, but also gather enthusiastic people and do positive charity activities.

Helping

Our purpose is to help students in need by variety ways because every student deserves to be treated well and grow up happily.

Opening

Our program is open to anyone. Not only open for people who search for help, but also open for whoever want to devote themselves for charity.

Sharing

As the saying goes, "Sharing is caring", we share the resources and love to those in need to show our concern. With efforts, people in need can have a better life and save resources.

Teaching

For students, there are usually a lot of questions raising in their mind. We invite experts in each subject to give courses. With this idea, we can also help the students who don’t have surplus money to participate related course or events.
