PURPOSE OF EVENTS

For most people, there are a lot of events which we can participate in our daily life, but most of them need to pay for registration fee. However, for some people, they don’t have such surplus money used for this kind of event.

This truth induce us to mimic and hold those event like summer、winter camp、speech.

We not only invite some celebrities or professional people to give speech about differeant topics, we also held some activities like, cooking class, knitting class etc. Through these events, we hope that students with some financial problems or have other excuses can still learn something that they can't learn it from school or their origin family.
